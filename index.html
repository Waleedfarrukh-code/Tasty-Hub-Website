Frequently Asked Questions

~Is Foodies Bread Really baked fresh each day?

yes! At Foodies, we bake our bread fresh every single morning. We never use frozen dough – every loaf is made from scratch so you can enjoy soft, warm, and flavorful bread every day.

No, we don’t. All our breads are 100% free from animal fats or products. We use only plant‑based ingredients, making them suitable for both vegetarians and those who prefer pure, wholesome food.
